The November 12 change

The law changes November 12. The flower on this desk already meets the new standard.

On November 12, 2026, a new federal rule starts counting a hemp plant's total THC — not just the THC it shows today.

Most so-called "THCA flower" won't pass that test. CBD flower will — and CBD flower is the only thing this desk brokers.

That date is written into every contract, so November 12 arrives with no surprises.
