How a Meridian deal runs

Four steps, in order, every time. The discipline is the product.

1

Intake & grading

Strain, grade, grow method, moisture, harvest date, seller licensing — logged before anything is offered. We verify counterparties against public license registries.

2

COA verification

Lot-specific COA from an ISO-17025 / DEA-registered lab, QR-scanned to the lab's own records. Implausible profiles and high-LOQ reports are rejected; large lots are independently retested.

3

Landed quote

Insured, hemp-specialized freight with the full document packet in the cab — COAs, licenses, invoice, bill of lading. Routed around hostile corridors. One number: landed.

4

Contract & sell-through

Specs as conditions, failed-COA remedies, defined risk of loss — and on THCA, sell-through deadlines with November sunset clauses. Signed before shipment, honored after it.

About November 12, 2026

Federal law redefines hemp on November 12, 2026 to a total-THC standard that includes THCA. We broker THCA flower only in states where it is lawful today, only with contracts structured to complete sell-through ahead of the deadline, and we are actively shifting supply weight to compliant CBD flower and pre-roll programs that continue after the transition.

We operate through public, state-lawful channels, verify counterparty licensing, and paper every load for interstate transport. We are brokers, not lawyers — nothing on this page is legal advice, and we encourage every counterparty to take their own counsel.
